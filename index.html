Welcome

I am a political science researcher working at the intersection of political methodology, emerging technologies, and public policy. My work focuses on causal inference, political behaviour, misinformation, and institutional decision-making in comparative perspective.

I am currently completing an MSc in Politics at the University of Oxford.

Research Interests

  • Causal inference and experimental methods
  • Political behaviour, misinformation, and disinformation
  • Institutions, leadership, and democratic governance
  • Synthetic agents and political decision-making
