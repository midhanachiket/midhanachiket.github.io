About

I am a political science researcher trained in political methodology, causal inference, and comparative governance. My work sits at the intersection of emerging technologies, political behaviour, and public policy, with a focus on misinformation, leadership, and institutional decision-making across democracies. My more recent work is pivoting towards exploring the usefulness of LLM-based causal inference tools in studying political science questions.
